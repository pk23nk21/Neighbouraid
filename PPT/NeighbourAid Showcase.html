02/WHAT IS NEIGHBOURAID
NeighbourAid03 / 10

Five ideas, finally combined.

Reporters post (text · GPS · photos · voice). AI triages on-device. Skill-matched volunteers within 5 km are paged over WebSockets — every alert cross-checked against witnesses, weather, and photo evidence.
— 01 · CORE

Hyperlocal Dispatch

WebSocket fan-out to volunteers within a 5 km radius. Auto-extends to 15 km on rare skill match (CPR, driver, etc.).

FastAPI · WebSocket · 2dsphere
— 02

On-device AI Triage

BART-large-MNLI zero-shot → CRITICAL / HIGH / MED / LOW. Runs in container.

$0/request
— 03

Multi-source Verify

Composite verified_score (0–100): witnesses + corroboration + weather + photos.

Capped per source · clamped 100
— 04

Built for India

Trilingual EN / हिन्दी / ਪੰਜਾਬੀ + 112/108/101 dialer + WhatsApp inbound.

en-IN · hi-IN · pa-IN
— 05

Free & Offline-tolerant

IndexedDB queue. Zero paid deps. Runs on Oracle Always Free or HF Spaces.

PWA · Service Worker
03/WHAT YOU GET
NeighbourAid04 / 10

Feature highlights.

Reporting / Volunteer / Map & trust / Communication.

Reporting

  • One-tap SOS · ~3 s panic-to-feed
  • Up to 3 photos, auto-compressed <300 KB
  • Voice dictation in en-IN / hi-IN / pa-IN
  • Anonymous tips · rate-limited 10/h per IP
  • Offline alert queue via IndexedDB

Volunteer

  • Live WebSocket feed + audio ping
  • Skill-aware routing (medical · CPR · driver…)
  • Vehicle flag + ETA broadcast
  • Trust score: resolved ÷ accepted (Bayesian)
  • Hands-free voice TTS for critical alerts

Map & Trust

  • Leaflet + OSM, 15-second auto-refresh
  • Heatmap overlay (72 h, urgency-weighted)
  • Community flag · ≥3 soft-hides alert
  • Auto-escalation: MED→HIGH 10 m, HIGH→CRIT 4 m
  • Witness button (within 2 km radius)

Comms & Reach

  • Trilingual UI + auto-translation cache
  • Native push via Service Worker
  • Share link / WhatsApp / QR fallback
  • Inbound WhatsApp webhook (Twilio / n8n)
  • Buddy ping → sms: / mailto: chips
